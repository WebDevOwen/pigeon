Pigeons!

Pigeons, the versatile bird.

Several people say that pigeons are the best birds. Pigeons have a really cool shape which makes many love them. Pigeons were domesticated by humans making them very friendly to us. Later though when they became less popular, the pigeons that relied on us humans, were left to live on their own. On this website you learn about the history of pigeons and what they did.

Their Homeland

The Homeland of pigeons.

Pigeons weren't always in America. They were native to Europe, North Africa, Western Africa. All the way back in the 17th century is when the were brought over. They were brought over by European colonists for food and communication. Now they are all around the US and a popular bird.
